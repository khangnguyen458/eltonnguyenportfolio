About Me

I have obtained a Bachelor's degree in Psychology, focusing on therapeutic interventions. Throughout my career, I’ve worked extensively at various intervention centers specifically designed to help children with autism. This immersive experience has given me not only a deeper understanding of how developmental disorders impact both children and their families, but also equipped me with practical therapeutic skills to help these children improve their daily lives.

On top of that, my passion for creativity led me to complete SEM1 at Arena Multimedia, where I developed strong expertise in graphic design. My training here has allowed me to blend two seemingly different worlds—psychology and design—into a unique skill set. This dual background means I can approach projects with both a creative and empathetic mindset, whether I’m developing visual materials or addressing more personal, therapeutic needs.

By combining the analytical rigor of psychology with the innovative potential of design, I believe I am uniquely positioned to create solutions that are not only visually appealing but also deeply meaningful and supportive for individuals seeking both therapeutic and artistic engagement.
